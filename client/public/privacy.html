Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

DOOT is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal data:

  • Generated Avatar: A random anonymous username
  • Email (temporary): Used only for OTP verification, stored encrypted
  • Public Keys: For establishing encrypted sessions

2. Information We DON'T Collect

  • Message content (end-to-end encrypted)
  • Call recordings
  • File contents
  • Real names or personal information
  • Location data

3. End-to-End Encryption

All communications are encrypted using industry-standard ECDH key exchange and AES-256 encryption. Only you and your chat partner can read messages.

4. Data Retention

We retain minimal data:

  • User accounts: Until you delete them
  • Messages: Never stored on our servers
  • Temporary OTPs: Deleted after 5 minutes

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties.

6. Security

We implement security best practices including:

  • HTTPS/WSS encryption in transit
  • End-to-end encryption for messages
  • Secure key generation
  • Rate limiting and DDoS protection

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Delete your account
  • Export your public information

8. Contact Us

For privacy concerns, contact us through the application.

DOOT Privacy Policy

Your Privacy and Data Security

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

At DOOT, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. This platform is designed from the ground up with privacy and security as core principles. We collect minimal data and employ strong encryption to protect what we do store.

2. Data We Collect

Minimal Data Collection:

  • Email addresses: Only for OTP authentication (temporary Ethereal emails for testing)
  • Avatar names: User-generated anonymous identifiers
  • Encrypted message content: Stored securely on our servers
  • Encrypted files: Stored in the Secure Vault system
  • Basic usage logs: For service monitoring and abuse prevention

3. How We Protect Your Data

End-to-End Encryption

  • Messages: Encrypted client-side before transmission
  • Files: Encrypted before upload and storage
  • Keys: Never stored on our servers
  • Transport: HTTPS/TLS encryption for all connections

Security Measures:

  • All data encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Limited access to production systems
  • Automatic data cleanup for inactive accounts

4. Data Retention

  • Messages: Automatically deleted after 30 days (configurable)
  • Files: Retained until user deletion or account termination
  • Authentication data: Deleted immediately after successful login
  • Usage logs: Retained for 90 days for security monitoring

5. Anonymous Communication

DOOT enables anonymous communication through:

  • Avatar-based identities (no real names required)
  • OTP authentication without permanent email storage
  • No tracking of communication patterns
  • No correlation between avatars and real identities

6. Third-Party Services

  • Ethereal Email: Temporary email service for OTP (no data retention)
  • MongoDB: Encrypted database storage
  • Socket.IO: Real-time communication framework
  • Cloud Hosting: Secure infrastructure providers

We do not share your data with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

7. Cookies and Tracking

DOOT does not use cookies or tracking technologies. Session management is handled through secure JWT tokens that expire after use.

8. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a security breach:

  • We will notify affected users within 72 hours
  • We will provide details about what data may have been compromised
  • We will offer guidance on protective measures
  • We will work with authorities if criminal activity is suspected

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data at any time
  • Export your encrypted messages and files
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Object to processing of your personal data

  • Report privacy issues through the app interface
  • Contact us for data deletion requests

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Users will be notified of significant changes.
